Submit

Feed the machine. If you shoot film and capture something that's all vibes — a corner, a sky, a moment with no people needed — drop it in. The machine is hungry and cozy inside.

Photos should be loaded to imgur at around a "medium" size. Don't go small or large. If I really like the photo and need it in another size and you provided your email, we'll figure it out together.
